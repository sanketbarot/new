❓ FAQs

Consider: desired income + expenses + taxes + billable hours. Factor in experience, skills, and market rates.

• Beginner: ₹300-800/hr
• Intermediate: ₹800-2000/hr
• Expert: ₹2000-5000+/hr
• International clients: $20-100+/hr

Realistic billable hours = 50-70% of work time. Rest goes to:
• Marketing
• Client communication
• Admin/accounting
• Learning
• Proposals

Hourly: Best for ongoing/uncertain scope
Project: Best for fixed deliverables
Retainer: Best for stable monthly income
