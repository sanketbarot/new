QR (Quick Response) code is a 2D barcode that can store URLs, text, contact info. Scan with phone camera to access content instantly.

• Payments: UPI, PayPal
• WiFi sharing
• Restaurant menus
• Business cards
• Event tickets
• Product info

Open camera app on smartphone and point at QR code. Tap notification that appears. Works on most modern phones.

QR codes are generated using public API. The data you enter is sent to QR Server API for image creation. For sensitive data, use offline QR generators.
