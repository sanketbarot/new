❓ FAQs

Markup is the amount added to cost price to get selling price. Expressed as % of cost. Markup = (Selling - Cost) / Cost × 100

Markup: % above cost
Margin: % of selling price
Example: Cost ₹100, Sell ₹150
Markup = 50%, Margin = 33.33%

• Grocery: 10-20%
• Electronics: 15-30%
• Restaurant food: 200-400%
• Jewelry: 50-100%
• Clothing: 100-300%
• Cars: 5-15%

Consider: competitor prices, target margin, brand positioning, market demand, value perception. Test prices to find optimal balance.
