❓ FAQs

Public Provident Fund (PPF) is a govt-backed long-term savings scheme with EEE tax benefits. 15-year lock-in with current 7.1% interest rate.

Current PPF rate is 7.1% per annum (compounded annually). Govt revises rates every quarter.

Minimum ₹500/year, Maximum ₹1,50,000/year. Can deposit in lump sum or up to 12 installments. Eligible for 80C deduction.

Yes! PPF is EEE (Exempt-Exempt-Exempt):
• Investment: Tax deduction under 80C
• Interest: Tax-free
• Maturity: Tax-free

Yes! After 15 years, you can extend in blocks of 5 years indefinitely. You can choose to deposit or not during extension.
