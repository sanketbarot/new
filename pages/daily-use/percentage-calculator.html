Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100
Example: 20 out of 50 = (20/50) × 100 = 40%

Result = (X × Y) / 100
Example: 20% of 150 = (20 × 150) / 100 = 30

% Change = ((New - Old) / Old) × 100
Positive = increase, Negative = decrease
