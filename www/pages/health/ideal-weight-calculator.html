❓ FAQs

Ideal weight is the optimal body weight for your height that promotes health and longevity. It's a range, not a single number.

We use 4 standard formulas:
• Devine (most common)
• Robinson (modern)
• Miller
• Hamwi (oldest)
Plus BMI 22 (optimal BMI)

BMI gives weight range (18.5-24.9). Ideal weight formulas give single target weight. Both are useful guides.

Use it as guideline, not strict target. Consider muscle mass, body composition, age. Athletes naturally weigh more due to muscle.
