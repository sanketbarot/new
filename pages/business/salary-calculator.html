CTC (Cost to Company): Total annual package including all benefits

• Basic Salary (40-50%)
• HRA (40-50% of basic)
• Special Allowance
• EPF (12% basic each)
• Gratuity (4.81% basic)
• Bonus, Insurance, etc.

• EPF: 12% of basic
• Income Tax (TDS)
