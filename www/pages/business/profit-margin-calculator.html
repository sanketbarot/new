❓ FAQs

Profit margin is the percentage of revenue that's profit. Gross Margin = (Revenue - Cost) / Revenue × 100

Margin: Profit as % of revenue
Markup: Profit as % of cost
Example: Cost ₹100, Sell ₹150
Margin = 33.3%, Markup = 50%

Industry averages:
• Retail: 5-10%
• Restaurant: 10-15%
• Tech/SaaS: 20-30%
• Services: 15-25%
• E-commerce: 10-20%

1. Increase prices (carefully)
2. Reduce costs (negotiate suppliers)
3. Improve efficiency
4. Sell higher margin products
5. Reduce waste
