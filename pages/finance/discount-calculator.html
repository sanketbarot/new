Discount Amount = Original Price × (Discount % / 100)
Final Price = Original Price - Discount Amount

Discount % = ((Original - Final) / Original) × 100
Example: Was ₹2000, now ₹1500. Discount = (500/2000) × 100 = 25%

Regular sales: 10-30%
Seasonal sales: 30-50%
Clearance: 50-70%
Final clearance: 70-90%

Compare final price, not discount %. "₹1000 with 50% off" (₹500) is better than "₹600 with 30% off" (₹420). Wait, do the math!
