❓ FAQs

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the average of all SGPAs across semesters, weighted by credits.

CGPA = Σ(SGPA × Credits) / Σ(Credits)

Common formulas:
• CBSE: CGPA × 9.5
• Most Universities: (CGPA - 0.75) × 10
• Anna Univ: (CGPA × 10) - 7.5
• VTU: (CGPA - 0.75) × 10

SGPA: One semester GPA
CGPA: Cumulative across all semesters
