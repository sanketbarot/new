❓ FAQs

National Pension Scheme (NPS) is a government-backed retirement savings scheme. Open to all Indian citizens aged 18-70. Creates retirement corpus + monthly pension.

NPS offers triple tax benefits:
• 80C: ₹1.5L deduction
• 80CCD(1B): Additional ₹50K
• 80CCD(2): Employer contribution
Total: Up to ₹2 Lakh tax benefit!

At retirement, min 40% of corpus must be used to buy annuity for monthly pension. Remaining 60% can be withdrawn as lump sum (tax-free).

NPS historically gives 9-12% annual returns based on asset allocation (Equity, Corporate Bonds, Govt Securities). You can choose conservative or aggressive funds.
