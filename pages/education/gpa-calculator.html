GPA (Grade Point Average) is the average of all grades earned, weighted by credit hours. Used by colleges to measure academic performance.

GPA = Total Grade Points / Total Credits
Grade Points = Grade Value × Credit Hours

4.0 Scale (US): A=4, B=3, C=2, D=1, F=0
10.0 Scale (India): O=10, A+=9, A=8, B+=7, B=6, C=5, P=4, F=0

4.0 Scale: 3.7+ Excellent, 3.0+ Good, 2.5+ Average
10.0 Scale: 8.5+ Excellent, 7.0+ Good, 6.0+ Average
