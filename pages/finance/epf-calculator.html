❓ FAQs

Employee Provident Fund (EPF) is a retirement savings scheme for salaried employees. Both employee and employer contribute 12% of basic salary each month.

Both employee and employer contribute 12% of basic salary + DA. Employer's 12% is split: 8.33% to EPS (Pension) + 3.67% to EPF.

Current EPF rate is 8.25% per annum (FY 2024-25). Rate is declared by EPFO annually.

EPF is EEE (Tax-Free) if withdrawn after 5 years of continuous service. Pre-5 year withdrawals are taxable.
