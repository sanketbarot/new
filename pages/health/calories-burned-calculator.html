❓ FAQs

Metabolic Equivalent of Task (MET) is the ratio of work metabolic rate to resting metabolic rate. 1 MET = calories burned at rest. Higher MET = more intense.

Calories = MET × Weight (kg) × Time (hours)
Example: Running (8 MET) for 30 min, 70 kg = 8 × 70 × 0.5 = 280 calories

High calorie burn: Running, HIIT, Swimming, Cycling
Sustainable: Walking, Yoga, Strength training
Best: Mix of cardio + strength + consistency

WHO recommends:
• 150-300 min moderate exercise per week
• OR 75-150 min vigorous exercise per week
• Plus strength training 2+ days/week
