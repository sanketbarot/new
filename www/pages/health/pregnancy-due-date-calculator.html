❓ FAQs

Naegele's Rule: Due date = LMP + 280 days (40 weeks). Pregnancy is counted from first day of last period, not conception.

Only 5% of babies are born on exact due date. Most are born within 2 weeks before or after. Ultrasound provides more accurate dating.

• 1st Trimester: Weeks 1-12 (organ development)
• 2nd Trimester: Weeks 13-27 (growth)
• 3rd Trimester: Weeks 28-40 (final development)

Schedule first prenatal visit around 8 weeks. Regular checkups: monthly (1-28 weeks), bi-weekly (28-36 weeks), weekly (36-40 weeks).
