❓ FAQs

Credit Card interest ranges from 30-42% per annum (2.5-3.5% monthly). Highest among all loans! Only charged on unpaid balance after due date.

Minimum payment = 5% of outstanding balance or ₹500 (whichever higher). Paying only minimum keeps debt forever and accrues huge interest!

Pay full bill before due date! No interest if full payment made within grace period (20-50 days). Set auto-debit to never miss.

If can't pay full amount, EMI conversion is better than minimum payment. EMI interest (12-18%) is much lower than CC interest (36-42%).
