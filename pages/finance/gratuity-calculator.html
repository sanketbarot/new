Gratuity is a lump sum payment by employer to employee for long service (5+ years). Governed by Payment of Gratuity Act 1972.

For Covered Act: (Last Salary × 15 × Years) / 26
For Not Covered: (Last Salary × 15 × Years) / 30

Yes! Gratuity is tax-free up to ₹20 Lakh under Section 10(10) of Income Tax Act. Amount above ₹20L is taxable.
