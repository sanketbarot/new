EMI Formula

EMI = P × r × (1+r)ⁿ / ((1+r)ⁿ - 1)

P = Principal loan amount

r = Monthly interest rate

n = Number of months

Smart Tips

  • Lower interest rate = lower EMI
  • Longer tenure = lower EMI but more interest
  • Make prepayments to reduce total interest
  • Compare loan offers before choosing
  • EMI should not exceed 40% of income
