❓ FAQs

Ovulation is when a mature egg is released from the ovary. It usually occurs 14 days before the next period.

The fertile window is 6 days: 5 days before ovulation + day of ovulation. Sperm can survive up to 5 days in fertile cervical mucus.

Highest chance: day of ovulation and 2 days before. Have intercourse every 1-2 days during fertile window for best chances.

• Egg-white cervical mucus
• Increased body temperature (0.5°F)
• Mild pelvic pain
• Breast tenderness
• Increased libido
