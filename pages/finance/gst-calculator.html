❓ FAQs

Common GST questions

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a single indirect tax for the entire country on supply of goods and services. It replaced multiple cascading taxes.

India has 4 GST slabs: 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. Essential items are exempt (0%), and luxury items may have additional cess.

CGST = Central GST
SGST = State GST
IGST = Integrated GST
Intrastate: CGST + SGST (50-50 split). Interstate: Full IGST.

Add GST: Amount + (Amount × GST%/100)
Remove GST: Amount / (1 + GST%/100)
