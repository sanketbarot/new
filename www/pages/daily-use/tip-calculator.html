❓ FAQs

Standard tipping:
• Restaurant: 10-20%
• Delivery: 10-15%
• Hotel housekeeping: ₹50-100/day
• Bellhop: ₹50-100
• Cab driver: 10%
• Salon: 15-20%

Standard etiquette: tip on pre-tax amount. But many people tip on total bill (post-tax). Both are acceptable.

Skip tip when:
• Service was rude/unsatisfactory
• Service charge already added
• Self-service/buffet
• Counter service
(Use discretion)
