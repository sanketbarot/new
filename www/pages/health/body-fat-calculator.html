❓ FAQs

Body fat percentage is the proportion of your body composed of fat. More accurate than BMI for assessing fitness as it distinguishes fat from muscle.

Men:
• Athletes: 6-13%
• Fitness: 14-17%
• Average: 18-24%
• Obese: 25%+

Women:
• Athletes: 14-20%
• Fitness: 21-24%
• Average: 25-31%
• Obese: 32%+

• Neck: Below larynx (Adam's apple)
• Waist: Narrowest point (above navel for men, at navel for women)
• Hip: Widest part of hips (women only)
Use flexible measuring tape, don't pull tight!
